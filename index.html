How it works

  1. Open the color picker and sample a hue via photo or camera.
  2. Save colors to a palette for your painting session.
  3. Open Mix Mode and combine colors to preview the result.
  4. Use the suggested ratios as guidance when mixing real paint.

Results vary by pigment brand and lighting. Compare under neutral lighting for best accuracy.

FAQ

Does Paletio work offline?

Yes. Core features like color sampling, palette management, and local simulations work offline.

Which paints are supported?

v1 targets acrylic. Watercolor and oil support are planned next.

Can I import by hex code?

Yes. Enter a hex value to add a color to your palette and mix alongside camera-sampled colors.
